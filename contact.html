Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We might already have the answer.

How long does a typical demo session last?
Demo sessions typically run for 45–60 minutes and are fully tailored to your industry and use case. Our solutions engineer will walk you through the features most relevant to your enterprise.
How quickly will I receive a response?
Our team typically responds within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call us directly at +44 191 000 1234 during our business hours (Mon–Fri, 9 AM – 6 PM GMT).
Do you offer a free trial period?
Yes! We offer a 30-day pilot program for qualifying enterprise customers. The pilot includes full support from a dedicated account manager and access to all features of the selected product.
Is my data secure when using AI-Solution products?
Absolutely. All our products are ISO 27001 certified and fully GDPR compliant. We offer both cloud-hosted and on-premise deployment options. Your data never leaves your chosen environment.
Can I migrate from a legacy AI-Solution product?
Yes, we provide a dedicated migration support program. Our team will handle the full transition from any legacy product (Nexus Assist v1, DataSync Core) to the latest solutions with zero downtime.
